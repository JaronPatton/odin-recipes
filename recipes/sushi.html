Sushi

A typical sushi lunch.

Sushi is vinegared rice with raw fish. However, raw fish does not always have to be included, as there's a wide variety. The fish is wrapped in seaweed. I like to dip the more basic of sushi / sashimi into 'Yum Yum' sauce and soy sauce. One has to prepare carefully when using raw fish.

Ingredients

  • Vinegared rice
  • Fresh and properly treated raw fish from the market
  • Seaweed
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Spread seaweed out in preparation for other Ingredients.
  2. Cut fish into half inch strips
  3. roll rice into strips and place alongside strips of fish on seaweed
  4. Roll and wrap ingredients with seaweed
  5. Cut rolle into smaller rolls for serving
  6. Serve with soy sauce and other sauces of preference
