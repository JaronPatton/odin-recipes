Strawberry Shortcake

Strawberry shortcake but with a cheesecake twist.

Shortcake with strawberry gel, strawberries, whipped cream, and sugar.

Ingredients

  • Shortcake
  • Strawberry gel
  • Chopped strawberries
  • Whipped cream
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Chop strawberries
  2. Place strawberries, gel, and teaspoon of sugar into a bowl
  3. Mix well
  4. Place shortcake onto plate and top with strawberry mixture
  5. Top with whipped cream
  6. Pour a glass of milk or favorite beverage
  7. Enjoy
